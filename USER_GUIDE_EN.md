# User Guide

## Account Registration
Open the registration page, enter your name, email, password, and role. Sellers must also provide store information. After successful registration, the application creates the account and starts a session.

## Login and Logout
Use the login page with your registered email and password. Logout from the user menu to clear the browser session and revoke the refresh token.

## Profile
Customers can update profile details and avatar. Uploads must be supported image files and must not exceed the configured maximum size.

## Marketplace Browsing
Use the product page to search by product name, keyword, or category. Apply filters by category, price range, condition, and sort order. Pagination controls move between result pages.

## Product Details
Product detail pages show images, price, condition, stock, seller name, views, average rating, review count, description, reviews, cart action, wishlist action, and WhatsApp contact link.

## Wishlist
Login as a customer, open a product, and select Add to Wishlist. Open the wishlist page from the user menu to see saved products.

## Cart and Checkout
Add products to cart from product detail. The system checks available stock. On the cart page, select items, provide shipping details, and submit checkout. Successful checkout creates orders and reduces stock.

## Orders
Open My Orders to view order history. Expand an order to view items and delivery address. Confirm receipt when an order is shipped.
